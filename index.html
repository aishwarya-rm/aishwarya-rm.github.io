Aishwarya Mandyam

I am a final year Computer Science PhD candidate at Stanford University working with Barbara Engelhardt and Emma Brunskill. I'm broadly interested in building tools for decision-making within healthcare settings. I've focused on reinforcement learning algorithms, and have a particular recent interest in policy evaluation. I am funded by a Stanford Data Science fellowship.

I am searching for industry and postdoc positions (with an emphasis on reinforcement learning and/or healthcare). Reach out if you'd like to chat!

Outside of research, I am an avid runner (and lead a run club called Race Condition Running at Stanford). I also enjoy dogspotting and eating black forest cake.

Machine Learning for Healthcare Symposium 2025
